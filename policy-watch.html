MoHUA Releases Smart Cities 2.0 Operational Guidelines

April 15, 2026
MoHUA issued revised operational guidelines for Smart Cities Mission Phase 2 (2026-31). Key changes: accelerated GIS-based city operations mandatory, integration with PM Gati Shakti for multimodal transport planning, mandatory Digital Twin frameworks for cities >1M population. RFP timeline extended to June 2026.
MoHUA Circular Policy Update

DPDP Rules 2025 Compliance Deadline: 12 Months from Notification

April 10, 2026
MeitY clarification on DPDP Act implementation timeline. Organizations processing significant personal data (>1M individuals) must achieve compliance by January 2026. GIS platforms collecting citizen data, grievance mapping systems, and property tax portals classified as Significant Data Fiduciaries. Non-compliance penalties: ₹50 Cr or 3% annual turnover.
MeitY Data Protection Compliance

Budget 2026: ₹950 Cr for DILRMP Phase II, ₹2,100 Cr for SVAMITVA Phase III

April 5, 2026
Union Budget allocation for land records and rural property mapping. DILRMP Phase II focuses on integration with registration systems and grievance redressal. SVAMITVA Phase III expands to 35,000 villages (UP, MP, Rajasthan priority). GIS-based property mapping and drone surveys remain core activities. State bidding now open via CPP Portal.
DoLR Budget Funding

DST/NRSC Opens High-Resolution Geospatial Data Portal

March 28, 2026
Bhuvan portal upgraded with 1m-resolution satellite imagery covering 50% of India. Free access for government agencies; commercial licensing available for private sector. Geospatial data liberalisation further accelerated under DST guidelines. Drone survey data ingestion expanded — civil survey companies can now contribute validated orthomosaics to national repository.
DST/NRSC Data Release Infrastructure

Smart Cities International Benchmarking: India Ranks 7th in Asia-Pacific

March 20, 2026
Asia-Pacific Smart City Index 2026 released. Singapore (1st), South Korea (2nd), India (7th) with notable progress in GIS integration and IoT deployment. Bhopal, Pune, Surat recognized for advanced digital twin implementations. Ranking reflects increasing maturity of India's urban GIS ecosystem and policy momentum from Smart Cities Mission.
MoHUA Global Recognition Smart Cities

PM Gati Shakti National Master Plan GIS Portal Phase 1 Complete

March 12, 2026
Cabinet-approved PM Gati Shakti GIS portal now live. Multimodal transport network visualization covering 97 lakh km of planned and existing corridors. Real-time project tracking, logistics optimization, and inter-ministerial coordination via unified spatial platform. 28 state governments, 15 ministries already integrated. City-level GIS systems can now sync with national DT.
Cabinet/DPIIT Infrastructure National Initiative

Maharashtra GIS Policy 2.0: Private Sector Participation Encouraged

March 5, 2026
State cabinet approves updated GIS policy allowing private companies to contribute spatial data to state SDI. Revenue department opens GIS data access through API. New procurement model: build, operate, transfer (BOT) for city-scale digital twins. Expected ₹500 Cr investment from tech companies in next 24 months.
Maharashtra Govt State Policy Public-Private Partnership

NSDI 2.0 Consultation Paper: Open Data + AI Integration

February 25, 2026
DST releases consultation paper on National Spatial Data Infrastructure 2.0 emphasizing open data, machine learning for map updates, citizen-generated data integration, and real-time sensor feeds. Comments invited till April 30, 2026. Expected notification: June 2026. Will supersede NSDI 2021 guidelines with significant interoperability changes.
DST Consultation Standards

Pune Smart City Eyes ₹150 Cr Digital Twin Phase 2 RFP

February 18, 2026
Pune Mahanagar Parivahan Mahamandal (PMRM) invites pre-bids for Phase 2 of Pune Digital Twin covering real-time traffic management, waste optimization, and predictive maintenance. Contract value ₹150 Cr (5 years). Integration with existing Smart City ICC mandatory. Bidding deadline: May 15, 2026.
Pune PMRM Tender Digital Twin

NSIC Empanels 15 GIS Service Providers for Government Projects

February 10, 2026
National Small Industries Corporation (NSIC) releases empanelment list of 15 GIS/surveying firms qualified for government contracts <₹5 Cr. Mix of startups and established agencies. NRSC training and certification mandated. Empanelment valid 3 years. Open to all Indian GIS companies meeting specified financial and technical criteria.
NSIC Procurement Vendor Management

Telangana DHARANI Portal: 50M+ Transactions, ₹8 Cr Revenue Collected

January 30, 2026
DHARANI land record portal milestone: 50 million transactions processed since 2020 launch. GIS-based property verification reduces disputes. Online mutation processing now completes in 15 days vs. historical 60+ days. State reporting ₹8 Cr additional revenue through digitisation-driven transparency. Model adopted by Maharashtra, Gujarat, Odisha.
Telangana DoLR Success Story Digital Transformation

AIBP 2.0: Water GIS Mapping ₹75 Cr RFP Across 4 States

January 15, 2026
Ministry of Water Resources (DoWR) announces ₹75 Cr tender for comprehensive water supply GIS mapping and utility optimization under AIBP 2.0 (Accelerated Inter-State Benefits Programme). 4 states: UP, MP, Chhattisgarh, Odisha. Contract includes drone surveying, network digitisation, and IoT sensor deployment for non-revenue water reduction.
