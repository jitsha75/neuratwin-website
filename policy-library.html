Central Legislation

Primary Acts of Parliament governing digital infrastructure, data, and geospatial domain.

18 Acts
Central Act

Information Technology Act, 2000

Foundation of India's digital legal framework covering cybercrime, electronic records, digital signatures, and intermediary liability. Amended in 2008 with sections 66A-69.

2000 (Amended 2008) MeitY 122 pages
Read PDF Compliance
Critical · 2023

Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023

India's landmark data protection legislation defining rights of Data Principals, obligations of Fiduciaries, and penalties. Critical for all GIS/spatial platforms handling citizen data.

August 2023 MeitY Rules Pending
Read PDF Impact Guide
Liberalised · 2021

Geospatial Data Guidelines, 2021

DST's landmark liberalisation of geospatial data policy — removing prior restrictions on mapping, drone surveys, and foreign entity access to Indian spatial datasets.

February 2021 DST 28 pages
Read PDF
Foundational

National Map Policy, 2005

Governs production, maintenance, and dissemination of topographic maps by Survey of India. Defines Defence Series Maps vs Open Series Maps classification.

May 2005 Survey of India / DST
Read PDF
Transparency

Right to Information Act, 2005

Establishes citizens' right to access government records including spatial data, GIS datasets, and project documents held by public authorities.

2005 DoPT
Read PDF
Telecommunications

Telecommunications Act, 2023

New legislation replacing the Indian Telegraph Act 1885. Governs spectrum, telecom infrastructure — relevant for IoT-enabled GIS and smart city sensor networks.

December 2023 DoT New

Operational frameworks, standards, and guidelines from key ministries.

NSDI

NSDI Guidelines & Framework, 2021

Defines India's National Spatial Data Infrastructure architecture — interoperability standards, metadata standards, and data sharing protocols for all government GIS projects.

2021 DST / NRSC 84 pages

Draft Rules 2025

DPDP Rules, 2025 (Notified)

Implementation rules under the DPDP Act 2023 defining consent mechanisms, data localisation requirements, Significant Data Fiduciary criteria, and DPBI framework.

January 2025 MeitY Notified

Cloud / Infra

MeitY Empanelled Cloud Policy

Mandates use of MeitY-empanelled Cloud Service Providers for all sensitive government data. Critical for GIS platforms storing cadastral, citizen, and classified spatial datasets.

2023 (Updated) MeitY
Read PDF
Smart Cities

Smart Cities Mission GIS Framework

MoHUA guidelines for GIS implementation in Smart Cities including integrated command centre, IoT integration, and city-level digital twin specifications.

2022 MoHUA 56 pages

Land / Revenue

SVAMITVA Scheme Guidelines

Operational guidelines for PM-SVAMITVA scheme — drone-based mapping of inhabited land in rural areas, rights-of-record creation, and property card issuance framework.

2020 (Phase III: 2024) MoRD / DoLR / SOI

DILRMP Operational Guidelines
