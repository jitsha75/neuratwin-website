Bid Pattern Insight
SVAMITVA Phase-III: Tenders show 60-80% weight on technical qualification (prior experience in drone survey, GIS processing, village-level work). Financial capability remains secondary — most successful bidders are NRSC-empanelled vendors or state GIS departments.
Bid Pattern Alert: Recent SVAMITVA and Smart City RFPs heavily emphasize prior government project execution. Vendor pre-qualification now mandatory for contracts >₹10 Cr. Private sector GIS startups increasingly partnering with established government agencies for credibility. Digital Twin projects showing preference for comprehensive platform solutions over point products.
